About Kone Café

"Coffeeology: Espresso yourself. So many blends, so little time. Take life one sip at a time, and stay grounded. Better latte than never. Take time to smell the cover"

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner : Chu Tuan

Opening hours : every day from 8.30a.m to 10.30p.m.

Address : 295 Kham Thien street , Dong Da

THE MENU

Crispy Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A delicious blend of white Cheddar and mozzarella cheeses on sourdough bread, topped with a Parmesan butter spread.


Cheese, Onion & Garlic Bagel

Make this your light and satisfying morning go-to bagel topped with melted Cheddar cheese, poppy seeds, sesame seeds, onion and garlic.


Hearty Blueberry Oatmeal

A warm, hearty blend of rolled and whole-grain steel-cut oats, topped with blueberries and a yummy medley of fruit, seeds and nuts, then finished with a touch of agave syrup for extra sweetness.


Grilled Chicken and Hummus Protein Box

Tender and juicy sous vide grilled chicken breast paired with roasted red pepper hummus and naan bread, accompanied with baby carrots and snap peas—a whole lot of protein to get you through your day.


Turkey Bacon, Cheddar & Egg White Sandwich

Sizzling reduced-fat turkey bacon and wholesome cage-free egg whites are paired with the rich creaminess of melted, reduced-fat white Cheddar cheese on a wheat English muffin. So good and surprisingly only 230 calories.


Strawberry Overnight Grains

Dig into your day with our vegan overnight grains that include oatmeal, quinoa and chia seeds, plus shaved coconut, strawberries and toasted almonds.

Caramel Machiato (H/C)

Freshly steamed milk with vanilla-flavored syrup marked with espresso and topped with a caramel drizzle for an oh-so-sweet finish.


Cinnamon Dolce Latte

Freshly steamed milk and cinnamon dolce-flavored syrup to classic espresso, topped with sweetened whipped cream and a cinnamon dolce topping


Caffè Mocha

Our rich, full-bodied espresso combined with bittersweet mocha sauce and steamed milk, then topped with sweetened whipped cream.


Decaf Pike Place® Roast

From our first store in Seattle’s Pike Place Market to our coffeehouses around the world, customers requested a freshly brewed coffee they could enjoy throughout the day


Matcha Tea Latte

Smooth and creamy matcha sweetened just right and served with steamed milk. This favorite will transport your senses to pure green delight.


Royal English Breakfast Tea Latte

A select blend of rich, full-leaf black teas from India and Sri Lanka sweetened with liquid cane sugar and topped with steamed milk and a velvety foam.
